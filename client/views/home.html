An open, interactive web documentary

Open, transparent views of Venezuela's political and economic turmoil

Why do Venezuelans have to queue before shops? Why is there so much insecurity and inflation? Why is Venezuelan politics so polarised? We create a web-documentary that explains the history and logic of Venezuela's problems!

Our format redefines documentary filmmaking. We fuse the best of journalism, social science, documentary filmmaking, and web programming to deliver the most intuitively comprehensible explanations out there on the web. Inventions, such as referenced subtitles and direct access to all data, will make this the most transparently researched documentary in the history of humankind!
